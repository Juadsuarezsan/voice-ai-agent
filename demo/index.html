Pipeline

How an input becomes a decision.

in: microphone audio ↓ step 1: Whisper tiny (CPU) — streaming chunks of 1.5 s ↓ step 2: VAD silence-end → finalize transcript ↓ step 3: Claude conversation (with rolling memory) ↓ step 4: TTS — ElevenLabs (if key) else local OS voice ↓ step 5: audio playback + transcript log ↓ out: spoken reply
